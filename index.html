Who is Ena Shinonome?

Shinonome Ena (東雲絵名) is a third-year student at Kamiyama High School who attends night classes. She is the illustrator for the underground music circle 25-ji, Nightcord de., going by the alias "Enanan."

Ena and her friends

Kanade Yoisaki

Fellow circle member. While online, Kanade came across her artwork one day and invited her to be their group's illustrator. Ena is grateful that Kanade saw worth in her illustrations and told her that their unit needs Ena's art, how it wouldn't be the same without her.

Mafuyu Asahina

Fellow circle member. Ena tends to be annoyed by Mafuyu's uncertainty, but at the same time appreciates her blunt and genuine opinions, as it helps her grow as an artist.

Mizuki Akiyama

Fellow circle member. The two get along well and often hang out together as they share many of the same interests. Mizuki also enjoys teasing Ena as she tends to easily get angered or flustered, often poking fun at her for being a "tsundere."

Mizuki Akiyama

Ena's best friend. They first met in middle school, when Ena stood up for Airi who was teased by their classmates for being on a variety show, leading to the two becoming close friends with each other. The two girls often share apps and photo filters with one another, and they also own matching things, such as the yellow cat cushions.

Honami Mochizuki

Honami is introduced to Ena by Kanade due to an upcoming job assignment which requires her to draw despite lacking artistic skills. Ena is shocked by Honami's drawing, noting its abstract nature and impact. Despite having quite the scare, Ena tries her best to help Honami become more confident with her art.

Futaba Natsuno

She is a very kind and hardworking girl who admired Ena's art style, complimenting that it's unique and even tried to at one point copy it, but was never able to do it properly. She attends the art school every day and even its workshops in hopes of entering the Tokyo Art University.
